Welcome to The Villainy of Zezzak

These chronicles recount the heroics, hijinks, and occasional flatulence of a brave band of adventures. Documented by Grimwald, whose patience for his companions is truly legendary.

Main Heroes

Eclipse

A charismatic & humorous paladin with a calm demeanor, known for her powerful blade and heart of gold.

Moothalamoo

A lethal wizard known for physics-bending magic, who turns creativity into unmatched battlefield power.

Zawn

A wise Moon Druid with powerful magic, mysterious grace, and the ability to restore life.

Grimwald

A grumpy dwarf with a knack for heroics (and complaints).

Valethor

A fierce fighter who leaps headfirst into danger, racks up countless kills, and makes chaos look like strategy.

Nym

A fearless rogue with quick wit, unmatched climbing skills, and a tattoo as subtle as his humility.

Side Characters

Derek

Eclipse's sweet and loyal boyfriend, a humble dragon-tender quietly hoping for his moment to shine.

Ollo

A magician with mysterious motives and an enchanting presence.

Fig

A quirky druid who keeps everyone on their toes.
